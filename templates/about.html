About This Platform

LoanHub helps users submit loan applications, get ML-assisted decisions, and review application insights in one place.

Quick Start (New Users)

  1. Create an account from the Register page and log in.
  2. Open Apply Loan and fill all required details correctly.
  3. Select the correct Loan Category, amount, tenure, and property location.
  4. Submit the form to get an approval result with confidence score.
  5. Go to My Profile to see submitted applications and decision explanation.
  6. Go to Analysis to view charts and summaries for your application data.

How To Fill The Form Correctly

  • Use real values for income, loan amount, term, and credit score.
  • Enter spouse salary as 0 if not applicable.
  • Choose valid dropdown options; do not leave fields on "Select".
  • Loan amount and term should be positive numbers.
  • Declaration field should confirm that your data is accurate.

Understanding Decision Output

  • Approved / Not Approved: Model decision for current input.
  • Confidence: Model certainty for the predicted decision.
  • Positive Factors: Inputs that supported approval.
  • Risk Factors: Inputs that may reduce approval chances.

Analysis Page Guide

  • Application-wise Loan Amount: Compare requested amount across your applications.
  • Loan Category Distribution: See where most applications are concentrated.
  • Property Location Split: Compare Urban/Semiurban/Rural application counts.

Security Notes

  • The app uses login sessions and CSRF protection for form safety.
  • Always log out after use, especially on shared systems.
  • Do not share your password.

Documents Checklist

Keep these documents ready before submitting your application:

  • Identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Voter ID).
  • Address proof (utility bill, rental agreement, Aadhaar with current address).
  • Income proof (salary slips, bank statements, Form 16 / ITR).
  • Employment proof (offer letter, employer certificate, business proof for self-employed).
  • Property papers (if loan type requires property-backed verification).

Improve Your Score

  • Increase repayment capacity by improving income-to-loan ratio.
  • Choose a realistic loan amount and tenure based on your income.
  • Maintain strong credit behavior and avoid missed payments.
  • Provide complete and accurate details in every required field.
  • Use correct loan category and location to avoid profile mismatch.

FAQ

  • Why was my application not approved? Decision depends on combined factors like credit score, income, loan amount, term, and profile details.
  • What does confidence mean? Confidence is model certainty for the predicted outcome; higher percentage means stronger confidence.
  • Can I apply again after rejection? Yes. Improve key factors and submit a new application.
  • Why does analysis look limited for new users? Charts are based on your saved applications. More submissions give richer insights.
  • Can admin view all applications? Yes. Admin users can access the admin panel and review all submissions.
